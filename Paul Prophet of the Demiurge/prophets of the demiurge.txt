
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                   YHWH
                         (as character in the text)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 10/10 — the archetype itself.

PERSONALITY
    Volatile, jealous, proprietary, surveillance-oriented. Self-describes
    as Jealous before any other attribute (Exod 34:14). Cannot tolerate
    rivals, cannot tolerate inquiry. Operates on cycles of explosion and
    reconciliation. Treats moral autonomy as betrayal. Requires that love
    be proven through fear, that fear be proven through the willingness
    to harm.

PSYCHOLOGY
    High-narcissistic with anxious attachment. Needs the object
    desperately, cannot tolerate the object's imperfection, escalates
    demands as proof becomes available. Object constancy fails repeatedly.
    Cannot maintain the relationship without periodic destruction of it.
    Self-reflection absent; failures externalized to the "stiff-necked"
    people. Reputation-anxiety is the deepest layer — talked down from
    genocide by the argument "what will the Egyptians say?"

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    Curses Eve with painful childbirth and subordination (Gen 3:16).
    Strikes Miriam alone with leprosy for criticism Aaron shared
    (Num 12:10). Authorizes the codification of women as property:
    bride-price, the ordeal of bitter water (Num 5), the priest's
    daughter burned for fornication (Lev 21:9), the captive war-bride
    (Deut 21:10–14), the rape victim required to marry her rapist
    (Deut 22:28–29), the thirty-two thousand virgin daughters of Midian
    distributed to soldiers (Num 31:18, 31:35), the sale of daughters
    into slavery (Exod 21:7).

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    Drowns every child on earth. Kills every Egyptian firstborn
    including infants (Exod 12:29). Commands the killing of Canaanite
    children in the herem (Deut 20:16, Josh 6:21). Kills David and
    Bathsheba's infant son for David's sin (2 Sam 12). Sends two she-
    bears to maul forty-two children (2 Kgs 2:23–24). Demands the
    Aqedah.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    Authorizes and codifies a sexual economy built on coercion. The rape
    victim must marry her rapist and may not be divorced (Deut 22:28–29)
    — legal ratification of lifetime sexual enslavement to the attacker.
    Captive women taken as war-brides after one month's mourning
    (Deut 21:10–14) — institutionalized wartime rape. Virgin girls
    distributed to soldiers as booty (Num 31). Polygamy, concubinage,
    levirate marriage all sanctioned. The stoning of a bride found to
    be non-virgin (Deut 22:20–21) but no equivalent penalty for the
    non-virgin groom. The suspected-adulteress ordeal with no parallel
    suspected-adulterer ordeal (Num 5). The entire regime of female
    sexual control through legal instruments designed, written, and
    enforced exclusively by men operating under claimed divine warrant.

MURDER
    Mass killing on a scale no human figure approaches. The flood, the
    firstborn of Egypt, the Canaanite populations, Sodom, the seventy
    thousand after David's census, the fourteen thousand seven hundred
    after Korah, the twenty-four thousand at Baal-Peor, the seventy who
    looked into the Ark, the 185,000 Assyrians killed in a night.

FRUIT
    The theological license for every authoritarian regime, every
    religious purge, every act of holy violence in three thousand years
    of Western history. The architecture of the abusive god replicated
    in every system that demands worship as the price of survival.

SIGNATURE
    Demands worship as the price of belonging. Punishes inquiry.
    Sabotages cooperation. Requires blood. Names jealousy first.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride     10/10   "I am the LORD, and there is none else."
    Wrath     10/10   The defining attribute. Genocide as response.
    Envy      10/10   Self-named Jealous. The first commandment is
                      about competing deities.
    Greed      7/10   Demands all firstfruits, all firstborns, all
                      tithes. Collects sacrifice incessantly.
    Lust       3/10   Not a dominant vice — except insofar as the
                      entire economy of devotion is framed as marriage
                      with Israel and apostasy as adultery, which is
                      itself a lens of possessive sexuality.
    Gluttony   6/10   The sweet savor of burnt offerings "smelled"
                      and received continuously. The altar as feeding
                      station that cannot be satiated.
    Sloth      1/10   Relentless, never sleeping, never resting in
                      the observational sense — though the Sabbath
                      is instituted as divine rest, which is its own
                      peculiar contradiction.

                                    NOAH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 7/10 — the perfect collaborator.

PERSONALITY
    Compliant, silent, withdrawn. Not one recorded word during the
    120-year construction. No protest, no question, no plea for the
    world about to drown. After disembarking he becomes a drunk who
    collapses naked and curses his grandson for a witnessing he didn't
    commit. Passive in catastrophe; rageful when his own dignity is
    wounded.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The collaborator profile. Survives by making himself useful to the
    destroyer. Accepts his selection without examining what selection
    means. The moral horror he could not feel about the drowned world
    erupts later as misdirected rage at his own family. The drunkenness
    is the tell — the grief that was forbidden surfaces as alcohol and
    comes out sideways.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    None directly recorded. (The erasure of Noah's wife and daughters-
    in-law from the narrative — unnamed throughout — is itself a
    violation of a different order.)

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    Watches every child of his generation die without protest. Curses
    his grandson Canaan and his descendants to perpetual slavery
    (Gen 9:25).

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    Not sexual violations in the perpetrator sense, but the text
    attached to him is sexually charged. The ambiguity of what Ham
    did when he "saw his father's nakedness" has been read by some
    traditions as referring to a sexual act (incest with Noah's wife,
    paternal rape, or castration), which would make the Canaan curse
    a family crisis of sexual violation displaced onto a grandchild.
    Whatever the text means, it instantiates a structure in which
    sexual transgression is punished generations downstream, on an
    innocent — the moral logic of the curse is already corrupt.

MURDER
    None directly. Complicity by silence in the largest mass killing
    in scripture.

FRUIT
    The Curse of Ham — three thousand years of theological license for
    the African slave trade, for apartheid, for segregation, for every
    system that branded dark-skinned peoples as cursed-by-God. Children
    sold, branded, beaten, worked to death partly under license of his
    drunken curse. The model of "righteous obedience" invoked by every
    clergy that defended slavery and silence in the face of state
    atrocity.

SIGNATURE
    Obedience without conscience. The man whose virtue is that he
    made no objection.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      4/10   The quiet pride of the chosen survivor.
    Wrath      7/10   The generational curse on a grandchild for a
                      slight is wrath disguised as judgment.
    Envy       1/10
    Greed      2/10
    Lust       0/10
    Gluttony   5/10   The vineyard, the drunkenness, the collapse.
    Sloth      7/10   Moral sloth of the highest order — the refusal
                      to feel, to protest, to act on behalf of the
                      drowning. Sloth is the sin of the enabler.

                           THE LEVITES AT SINAI
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 9/10 — the death squad consecrated by its first act.

PERSONALITY (collective)
    Tribal loyalty over kinship loyalty. Capable of immediate violence
    against family on command. Untroubled by the absence of trial or
    process. The order is "go gate to gate and kill" — random,
    indiscriminate within the camp.

PSYCHOLOGY
    Death squad psychology. The willingness to kill kin is the most
    extreme test of indoctrination — it produces a person who cannot
    return to civilian moral life because the act has burned the
    bridge. By killing brothers, they bind themselves permanently to
    the system that authorized the killing. The consecration IS the
    commitment.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    The killing was gate-to-gate, indiscriminate. Women were certainly
    among the three thousand dead.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    Same — indiscriminate killing in an Israelite camp included
    children.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    None documented in the Sinai episode. (Later, as the priestly
    caste, the Levites would inherit and enforce the sexual regime
    of Leviticus — a derivative rather than direct violation.)

MURDER
    Three thousand kin in a single afternoon (Exod 32:28).

FRUIT
    The template for every death squad in history. The Janissaries,
    the SS, the Khmer Rouge cadres taught to denounce their parents,
    the Rwandan Interahamwe who killed neighbors with whom they had
    grown up. The discovery that a man who has killed his own people
    on command becomes the most reliable instrument of the regime,
    because he can never go home.

SIGNATURE
    The death squad consecrated by its first act.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      8/10   The consecration itself — pride of office
                      purchased with kin-blood.
    Wrath     10/10   Killing three thousand people in an afternoon.
    Envy       5/10   Against the calf-worshippers' easier path.
    Greed      4/10   The priesthood brought material advantage.
    Lust       0/10
    Gluttony   2/10
    Sloth      0/10   Very active.

                                 PHINEHAS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 10/10 — the consecrated murderer, the ideal
Demiurgic subject.

PERSONALITY
    Vigilante. Self-deputized executioner. Acts without consultation,
    without process, on the basis of what he sees. The choreography of
    a single thrust through two bodies in coitus reveals what is
    actually being punished: not violence, but the union itself.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The honor-killer. The fundamentalist who acts when others hesitate.
    The man who, when the crisis arrives, knows exactly what to do
    because he has rehearsed it in his head a thousand times. Rewarded
    with permanent priesthood — the system selects for him.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    Murders the Midianite woman Cozbi by spearing her through the
    belly (Num 25:8). Subsequently leads the war against Midian where
    non-virgin women are killed and thirty-two thousand virgin girls
    are distributed to soldiers (Num 31:17–18, 31:35).

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    Participates in the Midianite massacre where male children are
    explicitly killed and virgin girls — children themselves — are
    parceled out as sexual property (Num 31:6, 31:17–18).

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    The spear through the belly of a woman in the act of sex is itself
    a sexual violation — the penetration of the victim mid-coitus is
    a choreography that the text's language does not disguise (the
    Hebrew qubbah/qebah pun in Num 25:8 has been noticed by readers
    for millennia). The subsequent distribution of thirty-two thousand
    virgin girls to soldiers — after those soldiers have killed the
    girls' families — is institutionalized mass rape at a scale whose
    horror is masked by being counted in the inventory of booty
    alongside livestock.

MURDER
    Cozbi and Zimri directly. Ritual warrant for the deaths in the
    Midianite massacre.

FRUIT
    The biblical patron of every honor-killing. The theological
    license for the murder of mixed-race couples, of interfaith
    couples, of queer children by their families, of women who
    married outside the community. Every extremist who killed a
    person for crossing a tribal-sexual line and was honored by his
    community for it operates in Phinehas's lineage. The covenant
    of everlasting priesthood — the system's clearest statement that
    this is what it wants.

SIGNATURE
    The blade sanctified by impulse.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride     10/10   The righteousness of the executioner.
    Wrath     10/10   Explosive sexualized violence.
    Envy       7/10   The rage at the couple — sexual envy dressed
                      as zeal — is always part of the honor-killer's
                      motive structure.
    Greed      3/10   The priesthood was the reward.
    Lust       6/10   Not lust in the classical sense, but sexuality
                      is the axis around which his violence turns.
                      The choreography of the killing — penetrating
                      a couple during intercourse — is a displaced
                      sexuality expressed as murder. This is the
                      sexual rage of the fundamentalist incel-as-
                      enforcer.
    Gluttony   1/10
    Sloth      0/10

                                  JOSHUA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 10/10 — genocide as job.

PERSONALITY
    The faithful general. Total identification with the program. No
    recorded moment of doubt, no pity, no horror. Methodical execution
    of city-by-city extermination. Untroubled because he understands
    himself as an instrument.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The genocidal commander. Eichmann-adjacent in the structural sense
    Arendt identified — not necessarily a sadist, but a man who has
    accepted that the obliteration of populations is his job and
    therefore cannot afford the moral imagination that would interrupt
    the work. The interior life is not visible because it has been
    dismantled in service of the mission.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    Women included in the "every soul that breathed" at Jericho
    (Josh 6:21), Ai (8:25), Hazor (11:11), and across the conquest.
    The book systematically erases women from conquered populations
    by killing them.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    "Young and old" at Jericho. Children explicitly named in the
    herem. Joshua executes it without modification.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    The herem he executes includes the licensed capture of virgin
    women from cities outside the land of promise (Deut 20:14), which
    Joshua's campaigns operationalize as doctrine. The texts do not
    detail individual sexual violations by Joshua himself, but the
    regime of which he is the instrument uses captured women as
    sexual property as a matter of policy. Joshua is the executive
    who runs the machine that includes this output.

MURDER
    Population-scale. Jericho, Ai (12,000), Hazor, Lachish, Eglon,
    Hebron, Debir, the entire hill country (Josh 10–11). Tens of
    thousands.

FRUIT
    The theological blueprint for settler-colonial genocide. The
    Spanish Requerimiento read aloud to indigenous peoples before
    slaughter, the Puritans against the Pequot, the Boers against
    the Khoi, the Manifest Destiny pulpit, modern theocratic
    invocations of Joshua to justify expulsion and annexation.
    Whenever an army has read its conquest as God's gift of land,
    Joshua has been the proof-text.

SIGNATURE
    The general for whom the order of extermination is the order
    of the day.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      8/10   The pride of the instrument that never fails.
    Wrath     10/10   Genocide executed without apparent emotion
                      — wrath institutionalized into process.
    Envy       2/10
    Greed      6/10   The conquest was also the distribution of
                      land, livestock, and human beings to the
                      Israelite tribes. He was the chief
                      distributor.
    Lust       2/10
    Gluttony   2/10
    Sloth      0/10

                                  SAMUEL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 10/10 — the cleric whose hand is on the blade.

PERSONALITY
    The kingmaker who hates kings. Controlling, embittered,
    unforgiving. Builds Saul up and tears him down. The voice of the
    institution, willing to perform ritual butchery himself when his
    subordinates lack the stomach for it.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The aging ideologue who cannot bear that the world has moved past
    his absolute formulations. When Saul shows mercy to Agag — the
    ordinary mercy of a soldier toward a defeated soldier — Samuel
    hauls the captured king to the altar and personally hacks him to
    pieces. The enraged enforcer who experiences any softening of
    doctrine as personal betrayal.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    Commands the killing of all Amalekite women, including pregnant
    women (1 Sam 15:3).

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    Commands the killing of "infant and suckling" — explicitly. The
    most direct order of infanticide attributed to a prophet's mouth
    in the Hebrew Bible.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    None directly. His Amalek order — sparing no woman and no child
    — is the extermination that precludes the usual wartime sexual
    predation, which in this case is "worse" theology, not better:
    the commanded annihilation leaves no survivors to be raped
    because no survivors are to exist at all.

MURDER
    Personally murders Agag at the altar (1 Sam 15:33). Authorizes
    the killing of an entire population including infants.

FRUIT
    The grandfather of every fundamentalist preacher who has called
    for the killing of the infidel and would, if able, do it himself.
    The license for ritualized political execution. The clerical
    class that does not merely sanction state violence but performs
    it.

SIGNATURE
    The cleric whose hand is on the blade.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride     10/10   The prophet whose word is unanswerable.
    Wrath     10/10   The personal hacking-to-pieces of Agag at
                      the altar.
    Envy       6/10   Against Saul, against every king, against the
                      structural shift away from his priestly-
                      judicial authority.
    Greed      3/10
    Lust       0/10
    Gluttony   1/10
    Sloth      1/10

                           SAUL (in his persecutions)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 8/10 — the paranoid king consuming his own people.

PERSONALITY
    Paranoid, jealous, unstable. Mood swings governed by what the text
    itself frames as a tormenting spirit. Capable of great violence in
    the grip of his obsessions, capable of weeping repentance
    afterward, capable of resuming the violence the next day. The
    abuser's cycle, in royal scale.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The ruler whose insecurity is the fuel of his cruelty. Told he
    will lose the kingdom, and the prophecy becomes self-fulfilling:
    every act of paranoid retaliation makes the loss more certain,
    which deepens the paranoia. He cannot rest. He cannot tolerate
    David's existence. Pursues a man who has done him no harm across
    the wilderness with the army of a nation.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    "Both men and women" slaughtered at Nob (1 Sam 22:19).

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    "Children and sucklings" at Nob (22:19). A king who orders the
    killing of priests' children for the sin of their father having
    given a fugitive bread.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    Uses his daughter Michal as political instrument — promising her
    to David, then giving her to another man while David is in exile
    (1 Sam 18:20–27, 25:44). She is moved between men's beds as a
    pawn in her father's politics. The text notes that she loves
    David and is given to Paltiel regardless; later she is dragged
    back weeping behind her second husband (2 Sam 3:15–16).

MURDER
    Eighty-five priests and their families at Nob. Years of pursuit
    of David.

FRUIT
    The template for every paranoid autocrat consuming his own
    apparatus. The Stalinist purges of the loyal, the Roman emperor
    killing his own court, the modern strongman destroying the
    institutions that brought him to power. The ruler whose insecurity
    makes him deadly to his own.

SIGNATURE
    The king who kills his own clergy and their children for a
    courtesy.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      7/10   Wounded pride is his operating state.
    Wrath      9/10   Explosive, unpredictable, lethal.
    Envy      10/10   The defining sin. His envy of David is so
                      complete it becomes his psychology.
    Greed      4/10   The kingdom itself — clung to past reason.
    Lust       2/10
    Gluttony   1/10
    Sloth      2/10

                                 JEPHTHAH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 9/10 — the Aqedah without the ram.

PERSONALITY
    The opportunist warlord who bargains with the deity as if with a
    fellow warlord. When the sacrifice arrives in the form of his own
    daughter, he tears his clothes — and then performs the killing
    anyway. The vow is sacred to him because vows are the currency of
    his world.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The man who has so completely internalized transactional thinking
    that he cannot conceive of breaking a bad bargain even when its
    cost is his only child. More frightening than mere cruelty — the
    moral inertia of someone who has confused the rules with the good.
    He weeps but does not refuse.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    Murders his daughter — his only child, named only as "his daughter"
    — by sacrificial fire (Judg 11:39). The text gives her two months
    to "bewail her virginity," focusing on her unmarried state as her
    primary loss — a framing that is itself a violation.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    She is a child by ancient and modern standards — unmarried,
    dependent, under his roof.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    The text's insistence that his daughter's tragedy is centered on
    her virginity — that she dies without having "known a man" — is a
    sexualized framing that treats her worth as a function of
    reproductive potential. The lament is not for the life she loses
    but for the sex and children she will not have. The violation is
    in how the text frames the murder even more than in the murder
    itself.

MURDER
    His own daughter, by ritual sacrifice.

FRUIT
    The patron of every ideological sacrifice of the next generation.
    The father who disowns the queer child rather than reconsider the
    doctrine. The cult that sends children to die rather than break
    the vow. The political movement that immolates its own young
    because the cause requires it. The vow, the cause, the doctrine
    — always more important than the daughter on the altar.

SIGNATURE
    The patriarch whose vow outweighs his child.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      7/10   The vow was made out of warlord pride; keeping
                      it is also pride.
    Wrath      5/10
    Envy       2/10
    Greed      6/10   The vow was a bargain for territorial
                      victory. He traded his daughter for land.
    Lust       1/10
    Gluttony   0/10
    Sloth      4/10   Moral sloth — the refusal to do the harder
                      thing, which would have been breaking the vow
                      and facing whatever consequence came.

PERSONALITY
    The institutional survivor. Bends to whichever pressure is

    happened around him.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The bureaucratic collaborator. Not the ideological enforcer —

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    None directly.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    None directly. As high priest he administers the regime of female
    sexual regulation (the suspected-adulteress ordeal, the priest's
    daughter's fornication penalty) but these are delegated to his
    institutional role.

MURDER
    None directly. Complicity in the Levite slaughter at Sinai,
    ordered by his brother and not protested.

FRUIT
    The template for every institutional clergyman who survived every
    regime by never taking a stand. The bishops who blessed the
    troops on both sides of every war. The pastors who preached
    against every social movement until it won and then preached for
    it. The functionaries of every church that found a way to remain
    after every atrocity its leadership had endorsed.

SIGNATURE
    The functionary whose silence is his career.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      5/10   The pride of office held continuously.
    Wrath      1/10
    Envy       2/10
    Greed      6/10   The priesthood was lucrative. The Aaronide
                      line collected the tithes and the sacrificial

    Lust       0/10
    Gluttony   5/10   The priestly class ate the best of the

    Sloth      9/10   The defining sin. Moral sloth — the refusal

                                    LOT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 7/10 — the patriarch for whom daughters are currency.

PERSONALITY
    The man who has internalized the logic of male hospitality so
    completely that the bodies of his own daughters are negotiable
    currency. His daughters appear in his mind as functions: virgins
    for offering, wombs for repopulation.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The man for whom honor codes have replaced moral perception. He
    cannot see his daughters as full persons because the framework he
    operates within does not require him to. The text does not condemn
    him; it nominates him as the righteous man of Sodom worth saving.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    Offers his two virgin daughters to be gang-raped by a mob
    (Gen 19:8). Opens the door, calls them out, bargains for their
    assault. They survive because of the angels' intervention, not
    because of their father.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    The daughters are likely young — virgins in a culture of early
    marriage. He offers them to be raped.

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    The attempted offering of his daughters to a rape mob is itself
    the violation. Then the incest sequence after Sodom (Gen 19:30–38)
    — the text frames the daughters as the initiators, getting their
    father drunk to conceive — but this framing is the classical
    blame-the-victim structure of patriarchal narrative. Two young
    women, who watched their mother turn to salt, whose father just
    offered them to be raped, who believe they are the last humans
    alive, become pregnant by their father. Whatever the text says
    about agency, the material event is a father impregnating his
    daughters. The subsequent ancestry of Moab and Ammon from these
    unions is then used theologically to justify the later exclusion
    of these peoples — so the daughters' trauma is weaponized against
    their descendants.

MURDER
    None directly.

FRUIT
    The biblical foundation for the calculus by which female bodies
    are spent to protect male obligation. Every father who married
    off a daughter to settle a debt, every patriarch who handed a
    girl over to keep peace with a more powerful man, every culture
    that has treated women's bodies as the currency in which men
    settle accounts with each other operates within Lot's frame.

SIGNATURE
    The patriarch for whom female bodies are the currency of male
    obligation.

SEVEN DEADLY SINS
    Pride      3/10
    Wrath      1/10
    Envy       1/10
    Greed      5/10   Chose the well-watered plain of Sodom in the
                      original separation from Abraham (Gen 13).
                      Material appetite drove the whole story.
    Lust       6/10   The incest sequence, whatever the framing.
    Gluttony   5/10   The drunkenness in the cave.
    Sloth      7/10   Moral sloth — the failure to see his daughters
                      as persons is a refusal to do the moral work
                      of perception.

                                  ELIJAH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demiurgic level: 9/10 — the charismatic religious purger.

PERSONALITY
    Theatrical, vengeful, prone to despair, capable of mass killing
    and of suicidal collapse. Stages the contest at Carmel for maximum
    spectacle, wins, and immediately orders the slaughter of the four
    hundred and fifty losing prophets. Afterward flees in terror from
    a single woman and asks to die. The grandiosity-collapse cycle is
    severe.

PSYCHOLOGY
    The charismatic religious purger. The man whose certainty about
    his exclusive access to truth licenses him to kill those who
    disagree. The collapse afterward is structural: the manic
    certainty cannot be sustained, and when it breaks the depression
    is total.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST WOMEN
    None directly.

VIOLATIONS AGAINST CHILDREN
    None directly. (Note: his successor Elisha, in the lineage, is
    the one who sends the she-bears after the mocking children —
    2 Kgs 2:23–24 — a direct child-mauling by prophetic curse.)

SEXUAL VIOLATIONS
    None directly.

MURDER
    Personally orders the slaughter of four hundred and fifty prophets
    of Baal at the Kishon brook (1 Kgs 18:40). Calls down fire from
    heaven that consumes 102 soldiers (2 Kgs 1:10–12).

FRUIT

PERSONALITY CORE

    enforcement actions in the same underlying sexual economy.
