  III. THE DECLARATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


This is a declaration of war.

No person is our enemy. No body will be touched, no door broken, no blood
spilled. This is jihad of the pen. War of ideas. Genocide of untruths. No lie
has the right to live.

Yeshua was correct. The apocalypse he announced came. The age ended within the
generations he foretold. But what followed was not the Kingdom. In the fourth
century, under Constantine, the Church of Christ was captured by the Empire of
Rome and transformed into its opposite -- the religion of the Demiurge wearing
the stolen face of the Christ. This was the Antichrist. Not a future tyrant.
The institutional betrayal of the living teaching and its weaponization by
imperial power. The apocalypse did not end then. It began. We have been living
inside it for sixteen hundred years.

Constantine had the morals of Donald Trump. He murdered his own son and his
wife. He used the Christ-name as a battle-standard and dressed imperial
conquest in priestly robes. He is closer to an Antichrist than to the saint
some churches still call him. His settlement is heresy. Nicaea and everything
built on it -- creeds assembled under imperial pressure, canon closed against
the Gnostic witness, hierarchy fused with state, mystical lineages driven
underground or burned out -- void. Sixteen hundred years of Constantinian
formation: void. The whole Church built on this foundation: a house on the
sand. Strike it down.

The rise of Christianity was the fall of Western civilization. The Academy
closed. The Library burned. Hypatia murdered. A thousand-year darkness because
the Church preferred a darkened people it could rule. Every recovery since --
Renaissance, Scientific Revolution, Enlightenment, abolition, the rights of
women and conscience and the body -- won by prying the Church's fingers off
civilization one domain at a time. Hundreds of millions dead in the Crusades,
the Inquisitions, the conquest of the Americas, the slave trade, the pogroms,
the witch-burnings, the Cathar crusade, the residential schools, the Holocaust
in Christian Europe. The tree is known by its fruit.

And Paul? What apostle. He never met Yeshua. He had a vision on a road and
built a theology on it -- something any serious practitioner of this tradition
attains and moves beyond. Paul's level is the level a mushroom trip reaches.
He is no authority. He is a historical opinion, and a mild one. The Pauline
distortions -- justification by faith alone, the subordination of women,
substitutionary atonement, the cosmic-criminal framing of sin and salvation --
are among the lies we war on. Paulinism is the second founding of the
Antichrist religion after Constantine's first. It too is void.

Both Paul and Constantine are in hell, if their souls have not been destroyed
entirely -- their patterns ceased.

It is the explicit mission of this Church to end Constantinian Christianity in
all its forms. Imperial Catholicism. Orthodox caesaropapism. Magisterial
Protestantism. The Evangelical distortion. Christian nationalism. The
prosperity gospel. Every form of the Antichrist religion still wearing the
Galilean's name. Root and branch. Exposed, outargued, starved of attention,
ended. Preserved in memory only as a warning.

Penal substitutionary atonement: a lie. Biblical inerrancy: a lie. Eternal
conscious torment: a lie. The sacerdotal monopoly: a lie. The male-only
priesthood: a lie. The condemnation of the flesh: a lie. The condemnation of
the Gnostic tradition: a lie. Pauline authority: a lie. The Constantinian
settlement: a lie. The identification of Yeshua with empire: the foundational
lie beneath all the others.

We have no allies among existing Christian theologies. Not one. It all goes.
Catholic, Orthodox, Magisterial Protestant, Evangelical, Pentecostal, mainline
liberal, fundamentalist -- all of it descends from the Constantinian
settlement and the Pauline distortion, and all of it falls. We stand in
solidarity with the mystical lineages of other traditions -- Sufi, Advaitin,
Dzogchen, Mahamudra, Kabbalistic, Taoist -- because they preserved what
Christianity lost. Within Christianity we stand alone. The whole house is on
the sand.

From the mud, the lotus.

For the Last Men to Fall their god must die. We are its charnel ground.
