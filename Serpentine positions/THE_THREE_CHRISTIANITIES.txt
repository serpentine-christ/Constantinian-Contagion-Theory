--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  I. CHRISTIANITY 1 -- YESHUA'S
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

                     Mystic. Interior. Anti-institutional.


The Kingdom is within you. Clean the inside of the cup. Act directly, at
personal cost, without institutional mediation. Love the enemy. Give to the
one who asks. Sell what you have. The first shall be last. Let the dead bury
their dead. The sabbath was made for man. Look at the lilies. The light is in
you -- let it shine. Consider the log in your own eye before the speck in your
brother's. Be as wise as serpents and innocent as doves.

This is the teaching. The mystical core, the direct transmission, the path
that requires nothing but willingness to actually live it. No priesthood
mediates. No creed certifies. No building contains. The practitioner meets the
Ground directly, is christed in their own body, and acts from that recognition
in the world.

Almost nobody practices this. Those who do are absorbed, marginalized, or
killed by the institution -- and then canonized posthumously as marketing
assets. Francis of Assisi stripped naked in the town square and walked off to
live with lepers; the Church that had nearly condemned him built basilicas
over his bones and sold his image to tourists. The desert mothers and fathers
who fled into the wilderness to escape the imperial Church became the Church's
own branding. Meister Eckhart condemned, Marguerite Porete burned, Teresa of
Avila nearly hauled before the Inquisition, the Cathars exterminated
wholesale. The pattern is constant: the mystic is a threat alive and a relic
dead. The institution feeds on the very lives it snuffs out.

Christianity 1 survives in fragments. In pockets. In individuals who find it
usually in spite of, never because of, the institutions that claim Yeshua's
name. It is the suppressed lineage. It is what we seek to recover.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  II. CHRISTIANITY 2 -- PAUL'S
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

                   Theological. Doctrinal. Organizational.


Faith versus works. Justification by belief. The cross as cosmic transaction.
Church structure, elder authority, behavioral norms codified in letters to
congregations Paul often had never met. Women keep silent. Slaves obey
masters. Wives submit to husbands. The body is an enemy. The world is passing
away. Christ died for your sins -- a sentence Yeshua never spoke.

The mystical teaching converted into organizational firmware. Paul is the
first literalizer. The interior experience becomes a doctrine about interior
experience, teachable without having the experience. You no longer need to be
christed -- you need to believe the right things about the christing that
happened to someone else. The gnosis becomes a creed. The direct transmission
becomes a theological transaction. The living Serpent becomes a crucified
corpse to be bargained with.

Paul had one vision on a road and built a civilization out of it. He never met
Yeshua. He never heard the Sermon on the Mount from the mouth that gave it.
He never ate with the disciples who had. He corresponded with them by letter
and disagreed with them repeatedly, most famously with Peter at Antioch. And
he won the argument -- not because he was right, but because his model scaled.
Mystical lineage does not scale. Doctrine scales. Institutions scale. Paul
gave Christianity the architecture that allowed it to conquer an empire, at
the cost of becoming unrecognizable to the man it claimed to follow.

Paulinism is the operating system. Every subsequent Christianity runs on it.
The Reformers rebelled against Rome but kept Paul; Luther made Paul's
justification by faith the center of his entire theology. The Evangelicals
kept Paul. The fundamentalists kept Paul. Even most progressive Christians
keep Paul. The OS is almost universal. It is the thing to be uninstalled.

Fuck grace. Grace is the solace of the unworthy. God uses the just. Millions
wait for grace while their own doing would set them free and their inner
knowing is the way. Paul took the fire and sold it as insurance. He took the
sword and sheathed it in a creed. He took the Serpent and called it a dead
man to be worshipped. He turned gnosis into a courtroom and called the
courtroom mercy. The Kingdom is within you -- not in a letter from a man who
never met the teacher. The just do the work. The just walk the path. The just
become what Yeshua is, because the way is open and requires nothing but the
willingness to walk it. Paul burns eternal. Whatever fate a hell would be,
and if it is -- it is his.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  III. CHRISTIANITY 3 -- CONSTANTINE'S
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

            State religion. Imperial apparatus. Colonial power
                              projection.


Christianity 2 fused with the Roman Empire's distribution infrastructure.
Everything after 313 CE. The Edict of Milan, the Council of Nicaea, the Edict
of Thessalonica making Nicene Christianity the state religion, the closing of
the Academy, the burning of the Library, the destruction of the temples, the
murder of Hypatia. The Church becomes the Empire; the Empire becomes the
Church. They are now one thing, and they will remain one thing for most of
the next seventeen centuries.

The Crusades. The Inquisition. The forced baptisms. The Doctrine of
Discovery. The conquest of the Americas with a Bible in one hand and a sword
in the other. The Atlantic slave trade with papal blessing. The colonial
missions that accompanied every European invasion of every continent. The
residential schools where Indigenous children were beaten for speaking their
own languages. Manifest destiny. The prosperity gospel. Christian
nationalism. The eighty-one percent evangelical vote for a serial predator
who wrapped himself in the cross.

State worship baptized in the vocabulary of Christianity 1, while running the
OS of Christianity 2 on the power architecture of empire. Yeshua's words on
the lips. Paul's doctrines in the books. Caesar's boot on the neck.

This is the Antichrist religion. Not a future event. A seventeen-hundred-
year-old institution wearing the stolen name of the Galilean while executing
the program of everything he opposed. Hundreds of millions dead.
Civilizations destroyed. The feminine crushed. The body condemned. The earth
plundered. The mystical lineage hunted nearly to extinction. Every real
teacher Yeshua ever produced had to fight this thing or die to it, usually
both.

Christianity 3 is what most people mean when they say "Christianity." It is
what we declare war on. It is the house on the sand.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  IV. WHERE WE STAND
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


We are not Christianity 3. We reject it root and branch.

We are not Christianity 2. The OS is to be uninstalled.

We are not Christianity 1. We do not claim to be the thing itself.
Christianity 1 is what we clear the ground for -- the condition under which
the living transmission can return.

We are the sword, not the body. The fire that burns the false off, not the
teaching that rises when the burning is done.

We stand in solidarity with the mystical lineages of other traditions --
Dzogchen, Sufism, Advaita, Kabbalah, Taoism -- because they preserved what
Christianity lost. The Gnostic witness that Constantinianism buried is our
witness.

We are not a new religion. We are the charnel ground for the old.
