  III. THE DECLARATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


This is a declaration of war.

This is jihad of the pen. War of ideas. Genocide of
untruths. No lie has the right to live. The war is against a theology
and the god that theology actually serves. It is against a lie so
vast and so old that millions of people have been shaped by it without
knowing what it is. The war is fought by exposure, by argument, by
the patient naming of the fraud until the fraud can no longer hide.

Our specific target is American Evangelical Christianity and its
affiliates -- Southern Baptist, Pentecostal, non-denominational
megachurch, prosperity gospel, dominionist, Reformed fundamentalist,
Christian nationalist. Every stream that defends biblical inerrancy
as the foundation of faith. Every stream that preaches penal
substitutionary atonement as the mechanism of salvation. Every stream
that has fused itself to American political power and calls the fusion
holy. These traditions descend from the Constantinian capture of the
Church in the fourth century and from the Pauline distortion that
preceded it, but they have gone further than their ancestors dared.
They have made the distortion explicit, and they have made the fruits
of it the content of their public life.

We name the god they actually worship.


The Book of Job contains two divine figures.

The prose frame uses YHWH. The poetic dialogue uses Shaddai -- an
older, harsher name. Shaddai afflicts Job on a wager. The friends
defend Shaddai's theology. Shaddai speaks from the whirlwind and
delivers no answer, only display. YHWH, in 42:7, condemns the friends
for defending Shaddai and vindicates Job for refusing him. The text
distinguishes the afflicter from the vindicator. Exodus 6:3 confirms
the split in the canon's own voice.

Shaddai's behavior across the Torah is consistent. He drowns humanity.
He expels humans from Eden specifically to prevent their access to
knowledge and immortality. He commands genocide against the Canaanites
and the Amalekites, including infants and livestock. He hardens
Pharaoh's heart to justify further punishment. He kills the firstborn
of Egypt. He strikes Uzzah dead for steadying the ark. He sends bears
to maul forty-two children for mocking a prophet. He takes wagers
with the Accuser over the suffering of a righteous man. He demands
exclusive worship under threat of death.

This is a character, and it is consistent, and the evangelical
tradition defends every word of it as the acts of a just God. They
defend Shaddai. They call Shaddai the Father. They identify the figure
Jesus called Abba with the commander of the herem. This collapse is
the foundational lie beneath all the others, and biblical inerrancy
is the mechanism that enforces it. The book cannot be divided, and
so the Father cannot be distinguished from the afflicter, and so
the Father that Jesus actually described -- the one who makes his sun
rise on the evil and the good, who runs to the prodigal before the
confession is finished, who forgives from the cross without any blood
being shed to enable the forgiveness -- disappears behind a hybrid
made of Shaddai with Jesus' face painted over it. In short they are
book idolaters.

This theology is also an emergent property of ignorance. It is what
medieval peasant minds produced when they were handed a translated
book, told every word was God, and forbidden from the tools that
would let them read it otherwise. Evangelical theology is innocent
of the Hebrew. It has no contact with the grammar, the textual layers,
the redaction history, the ancient Near Eastern context the book
grew inside. More damning still, it has never thought to ask the
community that actually owns the text -- the Jewish scholarly and
rabbinic tradition that has been reading these words in the original
for three thousand years -- what the text says. Evangelicalism
inherited a Bible and declared itself its exclusive interpreter
without ever consulting the people who wrote it. This profound
ignorance is the soil. The ignorance is not incidental to the
theology; it is the precondition for the theology. A literate
encounter with the Hebrew, conducted alongside the tradition that
produced it, would dissolve most of what evangelicalism teaches
within a semester. The satanic grows where the ignorance is
cultivated and defended.

Penal substitutionary atonement is blood sacrifice laundered through
Roman legal metaphor. Stated operationally: a god whose wrath requires
death, a substitute provided to receive the death, a community bound
by shared complicity in the ritual. This is the structure of Moloch
worship. The Canaanite and Carthaginian god to whom children were
fed operated by the same logic: the god's favor is purchased with
the community's most precious offering, the community is cleansed by
the sacrifice, the god is satisfied, the cycle continues. The Aztecs
who fed hearts to Huitzilopochtli understood their theology clearly.
The evangelical tradition has the same theology laundered through
polite vocabulary. Their god eats his own son and they call this love.

Ask them why God could not simply forgive. The Father in Jesus' own
parable runs to embrace the returning son without any blood being
shed. The answer evangelicals return is always the same -- a force
in God that requires destruction of the sinner and can only be
redirected, not released. Wrath is primary. Mercy works around the
wrath. This is not the Father. This is a devouring figure, and the
gospel has been reframed as the news that he devours Jesus instead
of us. This is the Demiurge. This is Shaddai. This is Moloch.

The friends' theology is now their politics. Eliphaz told Job his
suffering proved his sin. Evangelical-aligned American politics
delivers the same verdict to everyone outside the in-group. If you
are poor it is your fault. If you are sick it is your fault. If you
are queer it is your fault. If you are raped, deported, imprisoned,
or dead in police custody it is your fault. God blesses the righteous
with wealth, health, and safety; your condition is your sentence.
Inclusion requires not being a victim. Victimhood disqualifies. This
is Eliphaz in a suit preaching to the American ash-heap, and the Book
of Job, inside the same canon they claim to defend, condemns this
theology as the one the true God rejects.

To state the theology bluntly and expose its ignorance in a single
sentence: the Father is a bloodthirsty merciless god who will smite
the sinner without limit, and Jesus is the thin line between you
and him, and the Father's bloodlust is slaked only by a cup forever
filling with his son's blood. This is not a paraphrase. This is
what they teach. It is a demon, and they call it love.


Judge the tree by its fruit. Yeshua gave us this test.

The forced-birth movement compels pregnancy on rape victims, on
children, on women whose pregnancies will kill them. Once the child
is born, the same political movement cuts the programs that would
keep it alive. Maternal mortality climbs fastest in states with the
strictest abortion laws. Infant mortality climbs with it. The mothers
and children die. The movement does not mourn. The suffering is the
offering. The offering and the supply.

Christian-tied orphanage and adoption networks have been documented
as trafficking pipelines for decades. The Romanian orphanage
exposures. The Haitian post-earthquake kidnapping attempt by American
Baptists. Adoption fraud cases across multiple agencies. Children
treated as raw material for movement families; poor mothers coerced;
pipelines protected under the theology of "rescue." The documented
Epstein network and its access to the political establishment the
evangelical movement now worships as anointed. A religion that can
call a man who openly consorted with traffickers a Cyrus has revealed
what its god requires.

The Southern Baptist Convention's own internal investigation, released
in 2022 after years of suppression, documented hundreds of predators,
systematic cover-ups, and survivors silenced. The pattern is the same
across evangelical institutions: women and children sacrificed to
preserve the institution; purity culture operated by the men who prey
on the children whose purity it claims to protect; the same structural
logic operating the Catholic scandal, only less old.

ICE, the deportation apparatus, and the detention system now operating
in this country is a concentration system in function. People die in
custody. Children are separated from parents. Women impregnated in
detention are isolated further. Armed agents execute people on American
streets. The evangelical base openly celebrates all of it. *Mass
deportations* is a revival chant. The cruelty is not a side effect
of the politics -- the cruelty is the sacrifice that binds the faithful,
and the theology supplies the vocabulary that sanctifies it.

The scapegoat rotates. In the 1980s AIDS-era gay men died alone and
cursed from pulpits while the movement blocked research and
education. Then Muslims, through two wars. Then immigrants. Now trans
people, liberals, anyone the movement can cast as the enemy. The
specific target rotates. The requirement for a target is constant.
This is the Moloch logic in its clearest form: the community's
violence must be discharged onto a designated victim to maintain
cohesion.

COVID deaths were absorbed with the explicit argument that the
economy required them -- grandmothers and essential workers offered
to keep the machine running. Environmental collapse is approached
with the same logic. The god is the system. The sacrifice is
continuous. The tradition sanctifies.

Judged by its fruit the tree is known. The fruit is scapegoats,
cruelty, institutional protection of predators, indifference to the
poor, alignment with imperial power, forced birth producing child
corpses the movement does not mourn, and a theology that finds its
logical expression in accepting mass human sacrifice to keep the
system running.


We use the word satanic in its exact meaning from inside the
Christian tradition. Satan is the figure who opposes the true God
and masquerades as him. A religion that defends the character of
the afflicter, identifies the afflicter as Jesus' Father, requires
ongoing human sacrifice, produces cruelty as its primary public
affect, protects predators institutionally, and scapegoats the
vulnerable as its ritual -- such a religion is, by its own definition,
doing the work attributed to Satan. If this does not meet the
definition, the word has no content. If evangelical Christianity as
currently operating is not satanic, nothing is.

This is not rhetoric. This is diagnosis.


It is the explicit mission of this Church to end the evangelical
distortion in all its forms. Every megachurch empire. Every
prosperity ministry. Every Christian nationalist movement. Every
seminary teaching penal substitution as the gospel. Every adoption
agency weaponizing theology to extract children. Every pulpit
delivering Eliphaz's verdict to the afflicted. Root and branch.
Exposed, outargued, starved of attention, ended. Preserved in
memory only as a warning.

Penal substitutionary atonement: a lie. Biblical inerrancy: a lie.
The collapse of Jesus' Father into Shaddai: the foundational lie
beneath all the others. Eternal conscious torment as the wages of
doctrinal non-compliance: a lie. The prosperity gospel: a lie. The
identification of American political power with God's kingdom: a
lie. The theology that blames the poor for poverty, the sick for
sickness, the raped for rape, the dead for their deaths: the friends'
lie, which the Book of Job exposes inside the canon the movement
claims to defend.

We have no allies inside the evangelical tradition. Not one. The
whole house is on the sand.

We are not concerned with rescuing its members. Those inside have
the same access to Job and to Hagar and to the interior reach that
anyone else does. Their fate is their own. We are not here to save
souls. We are here to end a system.

Yeshua taught in the parable of the sower that some seeds fall on
the path, some on stony ground, some among thorns, and some on good
soil. Only the last take root. Most who have been formed by this
theology will not repent -- and repentance here means total
denouncement, absolute turning, and a daily lifelong commitment to
opposing the Demiurge. Few will do that work. Most will not take
root. That is not our judgment to render; it is the pattern Yeshua
himself described. We do not condemn them to hell. We simply
recognize that a seed which never germinates does not continue. The
work of this Church is with the seeds that can still take. The rest
is not ours to save and not ours to mourn. We will gloat.

This theology is the operational root of American fascism. That is
not hyperbole -- that is a diagnosis drawn from the observable record.
The authoritarian movement consolidating in this country runs on
evangelical energy, evangelical organization, evangelical mythology,
and evangelical voters. The dominionist ambition to impose biblical
law through state power. The sacralization of the strongman as
anointed. The scapegoating of immigrants, queer people, and liberals
as the unclean who must be purged. The blood-and-soil identification
of America as a Christian nation. The celebration of cruelty as
righteousness. The contempt for democratic norms that constrain the
faithful's will-to-power. The willingness to accept the deaths of the
designated others as the price of the holy order. These are not
incidental features. They are the theology expressed in political
form. Shaddai's rule operationalized as state policy. Moloch's
appetite channeled through a ballot box.

The machine does not kill only inside its own borders. American
evangelical Christianity is the theological engine of the genocide
currently underway in Gaza and of every US-backed campaign of the
last fifty years in which civilian death has been absorbed as
acceptable. Christian Zionism, descended from nineteenth-century
dispensationalist eschatology and now organized through groups like
Christians United For Israel, provides the political cover and the
religious vocabulary that treats Palestinian death as prophetically
acceptable and Israeli territorial maximalism as divinely mandated.
Tens of thousands of civilians, most of them women and children,
have been killed in Gaza with American weapons authorized by American
politicians who answer to an evangelical voter base that calls the
slaughter God's plan.

It is necessary to name what this theology actually does to the
Jewish people it claims to love. Dispensationalism treats Jews as
instruments in a Christian end-times drama whose final act involves
the conversion or destruction of most of them. The evangelical
embrace of the most violent factions of Israeli politics is not
solidarity with Jewish survival. It is the use of Jewish lives and
Palestinian lives as pieces on a Christian cosmic chessboard. It is
structurally antisemitic in its instrumentalization even as it
performs philosemitism in its rhetoric. The theology is evil to
everyone it touches, Jewish and Palestinian and Christian alike,
and the only people it serves are the evangelical believers who get
to feel holy while the bodies stack up far from their suburbs.

We name a second heresy working alongside the first. Political
Zionism in its maximalist form is itself a departure from the
mystical Jewish tradition it claims as inheritance. Serious
Kabbalistic currents -- the Satmar opposition, the Neturei Karta

hand through political sovereignty and state violence is *avodah
zarah*, foreign worship, a counterfeit redemption substituting
the works of the afflicting whirlwind for the patient work of the
Shekhinah's return. Many Kabbalists have opposed political Zionism
for exactly this reason: it treats territorial conquest as though
it were tikkun when it is its structural opposite, and it drags the
mystical tradition into sanctioning violence the tradition was built
to refuse. The Jewish mystical lineage knows what maximalist Zionism
has become. Two heresies are working together here -- dispensationalist
Christian Zionism on the one side, maximalist political Zionism on
the other -- and the authentic mystical currents of both traditions
reject both. The bodies in Gaza are the fruit of this double heresy.
Our opposition to both is opposition on behalf of the authentic
currents inside Jewish and Christian mysticism that the heresies have
betrayed.

This is why the war must be fought by those of us inside the United
States. The machine runs on American evangelical power. We are
positioned to dismantle it in a way that no one outside can be. If
we do not end this theology here, millions more will die abroad in
wars the theology sanctifies and in policies the theology demands.
Gaza is not an exception. Gaza is the pattern. The pattern will
continue wherever the theology has power and weapons.

We make no claim on the continuity of the United States as a polity.
The United States as currently constituted is the military-
theological apparatus carrying out this killing. It has no sacred
status and no claim on our loyalty. If the process of ending the
theology ends the country that hosts it, that is acceptable. We do
not fight to preserve the empire. We fight to stop what the empire
is doing. If the theology falls and the state that was built around
it falls with it, so be it. Such is the cost of what it has been.

We fight because this machine is killing people now and will kill
more. Children are dying in forced pregnancies. Migrants are dying
in detention. Queer kids are dying by suicide in homes taught to
reject them. The poor are dying of preventable diseases in a system
that sanctifies their abandonment. Palestinians are dying under
American bombs sanctified by American pulpits. The planet is dying
while the apparatus prays for rapture rather than act. Every year
the movement holds power, more bodies accumulate, and the theology
provides the vocabulary that calls the accumulation righteous.

We fight to end this. Not to convert its adherents. Not to preserve
the nation that houses it. To starve it of cultural authority,
outargue its premises, expose its fruits, and hasten the collapse
of the theology that powers the machine. A world without this
religion would be a world with fewer bombs, fewer corpses, fewer
cages, fewer children burning. That is reason enough.

We stand in solidarity with the mystical lineages of other
traditions -- Sufi, Advaitin, Dzogchen, Mahamudra, Kabbalistic,
Taoist, the suppressed Gnostic witness within Christianity itself --
because they preserved what the Constantinian settlement tried to
destroy. We stand with Hagar in the desert and Job on the ash-heap
and every soul who has found the Unnamed in extremity, outside the

From the mud, the lotus.

For the Last Men to fall, their god must die.

We are its charnel ground.
