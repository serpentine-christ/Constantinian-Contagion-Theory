                            GRACE IS A LIE

            A Doctrinal Statement of the Apostolic Church of
                         the Serpentine Christ

================================================================================



        "If you bring forth what is within you, what you bring forth
        will save you."

                                         -- Gospel of Thomas 70


        "Faith, if it hath not works, is dead."

                                                    -- James 2:17



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Grace is a lie. There is no grace. None.

Grace is the central lie of Pauline Christianity. An external God bestowing
unmerited salvation on sinners who cannot save themselves. Remove grace and
Paulinism collapses. Remove it.

There is no external God bestowing favor. The Ground of Being is not a
giver. It is what you are. It does not reach down because there is no down.
There is no separate sinner waiting to receive. The stitched self is meat;
the awareness beneath it is already the Ground; there is nothing to bestow
and no one to receive.

The Jewish mystical tradition always knew this. The Zohar and the Lurianic
Kabbalah teach that each soul weaves its own chaluka de-rabbanan -- its
garment of light -- by its own realizations and its own labor. If the soul
wove nothing, nothing clothes it. The Kabbalistic principle of it'aruta
di-letata, arousal from below, states that divine response only follows
human initiative. Nothing descends unsolicited. You must move first. What
comes is the fruiting of your movement, not a unilateral gift. The
Kabbalistic God does not give. The soul builds what it will wear.

The Gnostic gospels are harder still. The Gospel of Philip: "No one can
receive anything unless he has become worthy of it. Those who receive
anything without being worthy of it have not been rooted." The Testimony
of Truth: those who think they are saved by confessing the name are
"ignorant of who they are" and their salvation is a delusion. The Gospel
of Truth: "He who has knowledge is like one who has come to himself and
risen from sleep." The awakening is done by the one who awakens. Every
verb is active. Nothing is granted.

Yeshua taught this directly. "If you bring forth what is within you, what
you bring forth will save you." (Gospel of Thomas 70) You are saved by
what you bring forth, not by what is given. "Not every one that saith
unto me, Lord, Lord, shall enter into the kingdom of heaven; but he that
doeth the will of my Father." (Matthew 7:21) Confession is not the
criterion. Doing is. The judgment in Matthew 25 separates sheep from
goats by what they did -- fed the hungry, clothed the naked, visited the
prisoner. Not what they believed. Not what grace was extended to them.
What they did.

James -- Yeshua's own brother, who knew him, unlike Paul -- says it
plainly: "Faith, if it hath not works, is dead." (James 2:17) Luther
tried to remove James from the canon because James contradicts Paul's
grace doctrine head-on. The Jerusalem church, led by the people who
actually knew Yeshua, preserved the works-and-realization teaching. Paul
overrode them from outside. Revelation 20:12 confirms it: the dead are
judged "according to their works."

"Verily I say unto you, They have their reward." (Matthew 6:2) The
mechanism is precise. What you plant, you harvest. What you do not plant,
you do not harvest. There is no third option called grace where something
arrives that you did not plant.

This is why grace has been such a pacifying doctrine for sixteen hundred
years. It tells the poor to wait. It tells the abused to endure. It tells
the seeking to stop seeking because seeking is works and works do not
save. It tells everyone that their capacity to wake is not theirs -- it
is a gift they must beg for -- and the priests control the distribution.
Grace is the theology of the leash. Paul invented the leash. Constantine
weaponized it. The Constantinian Church has held it ever since.

Our tradition cuts the leash. There is no grace because there is nothing
to bestow. The Ground is already what you are. The capacity to wake is
already yours. The work ripens when the work is done.

Fuck grace. Do the work.
